Venue Rental Network

Facilitate connections between independent trainers and fitness venues, creating value for both parties without taking ownership of the transaction.

🏢

Facilitation Model

ANUO acts as a facilitator, not a broker: We connect trainers with available venues, handle booking logistics, and process payments. Trainers pay venues directly for space rental - ANUO charges a facilitation fee for the service.

Fee Structure
Facilitation Fee: 10% of booking value Covers platform maintenance, booking management, and payment processing
Venue Payment: 90% to venue owner Venue owners receive the majority of the booking fee directly
